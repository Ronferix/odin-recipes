Island Cherry Fizz

Description

The perfect drink for warm days! Sweet and tropical,
with a mix of refreshing flavors that come together
into this fun mocktail free of alcohol.

This drink has electrolytes and helps with hydration,
while the cherry adds inmense flavor and antioxidants.

Ingridients

  • 1 cup of ice
  • 3 ounces of lemonade
  • 6 ounces of coconut water
  • 1 teaspoon of tart cherry concentrate
  • 1 ounce of sparkling water

Steps

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice. Add lemonade,
    coconut water, cherry concentrate and sparkling
    water. Stir gently to combine.
